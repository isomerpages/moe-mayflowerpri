**MOE Kindergarten @Mayflower joyfully welcomes you with love**

MOE Kindergarten @ Mayflower shifted to our permanent site on 30 May 2022 and is currently at 200 Ang Mo Kio Ave 5, S569878.

#### **Programmes & Operating Hours**
**Our Operating Hours are:**<br>
MK: 8am – 5pm<br>
KCare: 7am – 7pm<br>
From 2021: K1, KCare<br>
From 2022: K1, K2, KCare 

**MK Uniform Matters:** [MK\_Uniforms](/files/uniform%20matters.pdf)

Your child's safety and well-being are important to us. [Learn how we take care of them](https://www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten/curriculum)

For further information please contact us at:<br>
Phone: 6255 2728<br>
Fax: 6255 2757<br>
Email: [mk\_mayflower@moe.edu.sg](mailto:mk_mayflower@moe.edu.sg)

The Kindergarten Care (KCare) programme will also continue to be held at this location.
